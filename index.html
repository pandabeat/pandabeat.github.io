Photographer

I've been shooting a Nikon DSLR for the past 6 years. I started with a D40, then moved to a D90, and now I'm shooting a D7100. I take my camera with me on all my travels. I am also "that guy" that takes pictures of his food before he eats it. I've run a few introductory photography workshops too. Check out my flickr.

Mobile DJ

It all started when I got my first DJ Tiesto CD back in university. My love of electronic dance music lead to the purchase of my first set of turntables. After practicing for a few months, some friends asked me to DJ their wedding. I had so much fun spinning at their wedding -- I decided to keep doing it for others.

Cyclist & Mechanic

When I was in junior high, a friend of mine got me in to the world of bikes. I started working at a bike shop as the kid that cleaned and took out the trash. It wasn't long before I became fascinated with the mechanics of bicycles. Soon after, I got trained as a mechanic. I wrenched on bikes for the following 7 years! I ride both road and mountain, and dabble in custom builds per special request.

Continuously Learning
